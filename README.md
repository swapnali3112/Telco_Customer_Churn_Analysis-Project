In the telecommunications industry, the cost of acquiring a new customer is 5x higher than retaining an existing one. This project performs a deep-dive Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on a dataset of 7,043 customers to identify the primary drivers of attrition and provide actionable retention strategies.

## ğŸ“Š Key Results & Conclusions
* Overall Churn Rate:26.5%
* Highest Risk Segment:Fiber Optic users (41.9% churn) and Month-to-Month contract holders (42.7% churn).

* Payment Friction: Electronic Check users are at the highest risk (>45% churn), identifying a need for automated payment incentives.
